MIXER

About MIXER

MIXER is a project in early developpement of two students that aims to display some of the most famous music albums from every genre,It is envisioned to be something of an album gallery, clicking one of these would direct you to another page with 5 of arguably the most famous/best songs of the album with an about page that has a brief description of the album.

MAKING

MIXER is developped by "ZIRARI SALAHEDDINE" and "MOKHTAR BENMOUSSA" using HTML/CSS/JAVASCRIPT with the help of SPOTIFY API.
