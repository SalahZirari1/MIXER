AM

Arctic Monkeys

Released September 9, 2013

Named after the band’s initials, AM is Arctic Monkey’s fifth studio album. Alex Turner said in an interview regarding the title of the album: As of December 2017, AM is Arctic Monkeys' most successful album, topping charts in several countries, and reaching top 10 positions in many more. In the United Kingdom, Arctic Monkeys broke a record with AM, becoming the first independent-label band to debut at number one in the UK with their first five albums.
