JACKBOYS

TRAVIS SCOTT
Released December 27, 2019

JackBoys is a compilation album by JackBoys and American rapper Travis Scott. JackBoys is a collective and group of American rappers signed to Scott's Cactus Jack Records, which is made up of Scott himself, Sheck Wes, Don Toliver, Luxury Tax, and Scott's DJ Chase B. The album was released on December 27, 2019 His new Jackboys album dropped earlier today (Dec. 27,2019) and Scott's main yellow car is a 1993 Mazda RX-7 Touring Edition owned by Jonathan Oregon of Enticed Motorsports. Super Street got the scoop on this one of a kind Mazda months earlier when they did a feature for the owner.
