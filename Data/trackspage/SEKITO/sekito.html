ティスティック・エレクトーン

Sekitō Shigeo

Released: Aug 21, 1985

Sekitō Shigeo (関藤繁生 / セキトオ・シゲオ) is a Japanese electone player. In the mid-1970s he recorded a four-LP album set titled Special Sound Series for Nippon Columbia.[1] Sekitō also performs and teaches as a pianist under the stage name Akishino Ken (秋篠健 / アキシノ・ケン).[2]
